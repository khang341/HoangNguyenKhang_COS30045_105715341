Exercise 6: Interactive Charts

This page presents two interactive D3 charts using television energy data. The histogram can be filtered by screen technology, and the scatterplot includes a tooltip.

Interactive Histogram

This chart shows the distribution of television energy consumption. Use the buttons to filter the histogram by screen technology.

Interactive Scatterplot

This scatterplot shows the relationship between star rating and energy consumption. Hover over a point to see the screen size.

Different colours represent different screen technologies.
